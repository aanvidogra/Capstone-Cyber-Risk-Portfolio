# Business Profile – Phase 1

## Company Name
Northstar Health Payments, Inc.

## Industry
Healthcare technology / payment processing

## Company Description
Northstar Health Payments, Inc. is a fictional mid-sized company that provides cloud-based billing and payment processing services to healthcare clinics. Its platform helps clinics manage invoices, patient billing records, payment transactions, reporting, and account support.

## Operating Environment
The company serves approximately 150 clinic customers and operates in a hybrid work environment. Employees use cloud applications, laptops, email, remote access tools, and shared business platforms to support customers and internal operations.

## Revenue Model
Northstar generates revenue through monthly subscriptions, transaction fees, premium analytics services, and implementation/support contracts.

## Core Systems
- Cloud-hosted billing platform
- Customer-facing portal
- Microsoft 365 environment
- Employee laptops and endpoints
- Identity and access management system
- Payment gateway integration
- Backup and disaster recovery environment
- Security monitoring and logging tools
- Vulnerability management platform
- Internal ticketing and support tools

## Business-Critical Functions
- Process billing transactions accurately and securely
- Maintain availability of the customer portal
- Protect sensitive billing and financial data
- Support employee access to business systems
- Meet contractual, regulatory, and customer expectations
- Respond to incidents without prolonged downtime

## Cyber Risk Relevance
Because the company handles sensitive financial and healthcare-related information, a cybersecurity failure could lead to business disruption, data exposure, financial loss, reputational damage, and loss of customer trust.
