# Cyber Risk Portfolio 

## Project Overview

This project is a multi-phase **Cyber Risk Portfolio** designed to demonstrate how cybersecurity risk can be analyzed from a **business perspective**. The goal is to connect cybersecurity threats, vulnerabilities, and technical controls with **real business impact** such as financial loss, operational disruption, regulatory exposure, and reputational damage.

The project simulates a fictional organization and evaluates its cybersecurity posture using the **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)**. 

## Fictional Organization

The organization used in this project is **Northstar Health Payments**, a healthcare technology company that provides cloud-based billing and payment processing services for medical clinics.

Because the company handles sensitive financial and healthcare-related information, cybersecurity risks could significantly impact operations, finances, compliance obligations, and customer trust.

## Importance of Cyber Risk Management

Cybersecurity risk is not just a technical problem. It is a **business risk** that can affect revenue, legal compliance, operational continuity, and reputation. Organizations must understand how cyber threats translate into real business consequences in order to prioritize investments and controls effectively.

This project demonstrates how cyber risk can be documented and communicated in a structured and business-focused way.
