# Risk Analysis Summary

The cybersecurity risk analysis identified several high-priority risks affecting the most critical assets of Northstar Health Payments. The highest ranked risks include SQL injection attacks targeting the patient billing web portal, ransomware threats affecting sensitive financial records, malware infections on employee workstations, and phishing attacks delivered through the corporate email system.

These risks received the highest scores because they combine both high likelihood and severe potential business impact. If exploited, these vulnerabilities could lead to data breaches, operational disruption, financial loss, and reputational damage for the organization.

The risk heat map highlights that web application security, employee security awareness, endpoint protection, and database access controls represent the most critical areas that require attention in future cybersecurity improvements. Addressing these risks will be essential for protecting sensitive financial data and ensuring continued service availability for healthcare clients.
